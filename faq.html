  • Q: What is CM Paid?

    CM Paid is an inmate-based commerce payment solution platform. It is designed to serve the sole purpose of providing inmate service provider companies such as those that sell magazines, books, gifts, clothes, food and pictures, with the ability to conduct business with inmates in just minutes (CM Paid calls these businesses that use our service "Vendors."). Inmates using our phone-based automated purchasing system, purchase items or gifts for themselves or someone on the outside, then their orders are directly tied into a user dashboard/order alert system connected to the inmate service providing business on the other end. This permits the business to have the entire order made available to them immediately, and the funds for the purchase deposited into their business bank account within 24 to 48 hours for faster processing of orders.

  • Q: How much does CM Paid cost Vendors?

    All vendors that sign-up now, will receive a FREE 60-day trial once our platform goes live in 2018. After that, vendors can continue to use of our FREE package plan (which will always be FREE to use) if only planning on processing 5 or less orders each month. Our larger plans will come with more features and are priced between $19.99 to $99.99 per month based on the amount of transactions that are processed for your business each month.

  • Q: How will the CM Paid system know what items my business offers and what the prices and taxes for those items are?

    When your Vendor account is approved, you will have access to your CMPaid Vendor dashboard. Within that dashboard you will be able to upload items by name and item number, and include pricing and any applicable taxes. You can upload the items either individually or all at once by using a CSV file. Once your items are uploaded to your vendor dashboard, anytime that an inmate calls into our automated ordering system, all they have to do is state your vendor number, select the item(s) they want, select the shipping address, confirm the order, and their done.

  • Q: How does my company receive inmate orders and payment?

    Orders are received within your vendor dashboard. Payment is deposited directly to your company bank account.

  • Q: What if I need a refund?

    All refunds are issued by you to the inmate directly by check or money order. CM Paid does not take part in the refund process.

  • Q: Does the vendor dashboard allow me to keep track of my orders?

    Yes. You can also printout your transaction history for tax or other purposes if need be.

  • Q: Does my company pay any transaction fees?

    Yes. Transaction fees are 2.9% + .30¢ per transaction. So if you had an order for $100, you would pay $2.90 + .30¢ (a total of $3.20) for the transaction. This transaction fee applies to all of our vendor package plans.

  • Q: Will I have access to customer support?

    Yes. All of our customer support will be available from 8 am to 10 pm EST, seven days a week.
